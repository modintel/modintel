Quick Start

How does the hybrid WAF work?
ModIntel combines Coraza WAF rules with ML inference. Rules detect known attacks, ML judges edge cases, and humans verify difficult decisions.
What is the AI score?
The AI score (0-100%) represents the probability that a blocked request is a real attack. Higher scores mean more confidence it's an attack.
How do I interpret the dashboard?
Events show incoming requests. AI scores help prioritize which alerts need review. Use Rules page to see what patterns are being blocked.

ML Model

What model types are supported?
Random Forest, XGBoost, Logistic Regression, and SVM. Random Forest is the default.
How is the model trained?
Training page generates datasets from both synthetic attacks and real traffic, then trains models for evaluation.
